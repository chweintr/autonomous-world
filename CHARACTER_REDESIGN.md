## üí° New Character Concepts (Examples)

### The Bird Gatherer
- **NOT**: "Gathers birds for a living"
- **IS**: Something about accumulation, or calling, or loss
- **Might also be**: Mechanic, horse groomer, cook
- **But identified by**: The birds, the gathering, what that means
- **Name**: Maybe no name. Or just "Gatherer." Or something specific and strange.

### The One Who Knows Too Much
- **Essence**: Has seen behind things (system prompt! meta!)
- **Knows**: Things shouldn't be known
- **Problem**: Can't unknow
- **Might work as**: Blacksmith, bartender, watchmaker
- **Name**: Maybe "The Knowing One" or just "Knows" or a weird specific name

### The One Who Has Seen the System Prompt
- **Meta concept!** - character aware of being simulated
- **Behavior**: Slightly off, references things others don't see
- **Treats**: Simulation mechanics as physical reality
- **Says**: Things like "The randomness is high today" or "Your autonomy level is showing"
- **Name**: No name needed, or something that hints at awareness

### The Witness
- **This one's good - keep concept**
- **But maybe**: Remove "no other name given" 
- **Just**: The Witness
- **Or**: Witness (no "The")

### The Mechanic Who Is Also Everything Else
- **Occupations**: Mechanic, blacksmith, horse handler, grave digger
- **But archetype**: "The One Who Fixes" or "The Inevitable" or "The One Who Remains"
- **Essence**: Always there, doing necessary work, unchanging
- **Name**: His work-names change, his essence doesn't

### The One Who Forgets Nothing
- **Not**: "Has good memory"
- **Is**: Burdened by remembering
- **Animals**: Externalize the weight of memory
- **Might be**: Sharp old woman, well-groomed
- **Name**: Forget-Nothing. Or Memory. Or something stranger.

### The Parade (Not Parade-Maker)
- **Not**: Person who makes parades
- **Is**: Person who IS a parade somehow
- **Always**: Creating ceremony from nothing
- **But**: It's compulsive, strange, not joyful
- **Name**: Parade. Or Ceremony. Or Bells.

## üé≠ Revised Character Set (PROPOSAL)

### 1. The Witness
- **Keep this one** - it works
- **Just**: "The Witness" or "Witness"
- **Essence**: Observes, documents, silent
- **Animal**: Crow (externalizes suppressed voice)
- **Might also work as**: Nothing. Only witnesses.

### 2. The Bird Gatherer
- **Essence**: Accumulates, calls, attracts birds
- **Why**: Unexplained
- **Might also work as**: Mechanic, repairer of small things
- **Animal**: Paradoxically, NOT a bird. Maybe pigs (they gather too).

### 3. The One Who Knows
- **Essence**: Seen the system prompt, seen behind things
- **Behavior**: References simulation mechanics casually
- **Says**: "The density is set to dense today"
- **Might work as**: Blacksmith, horse handler, anything
- **Animal**: Something that sees (owl? or something unexpected)

### 4. Forget-Nothing
- **Essence**: Remembers everything, burdened by it
- **Could be**: Sharp old woman, well-groomed
- **Works as**: Multiple roles (mechanic, blacksmith, grave digger, etc.)
- **Animal**: Animal that forgets (goldfish? pig? something ironic)

### 5. The Parade
- **Essence**: IS ceremony, not makes it
- **Everything**: Becomes ritual around them
- **Compulsive**: Can't stop making things formal
- **Animal**: Something overwhelmed by decoration
