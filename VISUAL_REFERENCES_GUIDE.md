### Image Formats

**Accepted:**
- `.jpg` / `.jpeg` (most common)
- `.png` (if you have them)
- `.tif` / `.tiff` (high quality scans)
- `.webp` (modern format)

**Resolution:**
- **Minimum**: 512x512 (for AI analysis)
- **Good**: 1024x1024 or larger
- **Best**: Original resolution of your work

**Don't resize unless huge** - system will handle it.

### How Many Images?

**Minimum to start:**
- 5-10 reference works (your paintings)
- 10-20 character/location studies
- Total: 15-30 images

**Good starting point:**
- 10-20 reference works
- 30-50 character/location/atmosphere
- Total: 40-70 images

**Comprehensive:**
- All relevant work you have
- 100+ images fine
- More = better understanding of your style

**Start small, add more as you go.**
